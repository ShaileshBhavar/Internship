Among the Best Pune Hotels for Business & Leisure Travellers

The O dedicates two floors for business, private lounges and entertainment. Set in a personalized and stylish manner, the O is the place to be for 'business'.

Rectange & Square Banquet Hall

The hotel provides extensive banqueting space spread over 3600 square feet, including three private rooms and an opulent, column-less Ballroom. The Ballroom is connected with spacious pre-function areas. The space can accommodate round table seating for 160 people and theater style arrangement for 400 people.

Lobby Level Hall

State of the art Banquet Hall at Lobby Level with Unique natural sunlight spread over 2600 sq. ft. and separate dining area with capacity of 100 in round table & 200 in theatre seating.

Trambkeshwar

Trimbakeshwar is a famous Hindu pilgrimage place located near Nashik, Maharashtra. It is one of the 12 Jyotirlingas of Lord Shiva. The Godavari River originates from Trimbakeshwar. The temple is known for its unique three-faced Shiva Lingam, representing Brahma, Vishnu, and Mahesh.
